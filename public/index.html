Why Stratus Auto

Premium car protection, without the dealership markup.

A real custom-fit cover used to mean six-week lead times from a body shop. We rebuilt the process so you can order a VIN-matched cover online and have it on your car in 7–10 days — at honest, transparent pricing.

VIN-matched custom fit. Every cover is laser-patterned to your exact year, make and model. No more loose-fit universal covers that flap in the wind — your cover is engineered for your car.

Marine-grade weatherproof fabrics. We use 4-layer Stormweave shell with UV-reflective coating, soft microfleece liner, hail-resistant padding and double-locked marine-grade stitching — built for snow, rain and 110°F driveways.

Shipped in 7–10 business days. Once we confirm your VIN and color choice, our workshop team begins cutting and stitching immediately. Every cover is fit-tested on a reference template, inspected and folded by hand before delivery.

5-year fit & weather warranty. Use your cover every day, in any weather. If a seam, coating or strap fails within five years we will repair or remake it free of charge — or refund you in full, no questions asked.

Our Workshop

An auto-cover atelier in the heart of the Rockies.

Stratus Auto Covers started in 2019 when two textile artists — Clara Vance and Sophie Holt — left fast fashion to make slow, intentional crochet wear for real bodies. They opened a 2,800 sq ft studio on Pearl Street with one rule: every stitch must be made by hand, every fiber must be traceable, and every piece must fit like it was made just for you.

Six years and 14,000 covers later, that rule still holds. Every cover is patterned, stitched, fit-tested and finished by our team so we can repair it for life — no matter when you bought it.
